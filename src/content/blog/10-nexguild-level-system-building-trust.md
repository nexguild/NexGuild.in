Every contributor on NexGuild starts at the same place — Level 1, with access to the platform's entry-level opportunities. What happens after that is entirely shaped by your own activity. This post explains how the Level and XP system works, why we built it this way, and how to think about growing within it.

## Why a Level System Exists

Not all tasks are equal. Some are simple and quick; others involve more complexity, more responsibility, or require a higher degree of trust in the contributor completing them. Rather than opening every task to every contributor regardless of experience, we use a level system to match contributors with opportunities appropriate to their demonstrated track record on the platform.

This benefits everyone. New contributors get a clear, achievable path to start earning immediately on entry-level tasks. Experienced, reliable contributors get access to higher-value opportunities as a direct result of their consistency. And the overall quality of work submitted across the platform stays higher, because access to more demanding tasks is tied to a real track record.

## How XP Works

Every approved task awards XP (experience points) in addition to NexCoins. The amount of XP a task awards is set individually for each task — it's not always directly tied to the coin payout, since some tasks are weighted to reward XP more heavily based on their complexity or the trust required to complete them well.

Your XP accumulates over time, and your Level is calculated directly from your total XP: each level requires more cumulative XP than the last, so progress naturally compounds — leveling up gets more meaningful, not just numerically bigger, the further you go.

## How Levels Affect Task Access

Each task on NexGuild can have a minimum level requirement set by our team. If your current level meets or exceeds a task's requirement, you can start it immediately. If it doesn't, you'll still see the task on the Opportunities board — it'll simply show a locked badge with the level requirement clearly displayed.

We chose this approach deliberately. Rather than hiding higher-level tasks from newer contributors entirely, we keep them visible with a clear requirement attached. This means you always know what's ahead and exactly what you're working toward, instead of opportunities feeling arbitrary or hidden.

## How to Grow Your Level Efficiently

**Focus on consistency, not just volume.** Approved tasks are what generates XP — a rejected submission earns nothing. Taking the time to follow instructions carefully and submit quality work is a faster path to leveling up than rushing through many tasks with a high rejection rate.

**Pay attention to XP-weighted tasks.** Since XP and coin rewards are set independently per task, some tasks offer disproportionately good XP relative to their coin payout. These are worth prioritizing if leveling up quickly is your current goal.

**Build a daily habit.** NexGuild's daily streak system rewards consistent activity — completing at least one approved task each day keeps your streak alive and unlocks bonus NexCoins, with a larger bonus on day seven. Beyond the direct reward, consistent daily activity naturally compounds your XP over time as well.

## What Your Level Says About You

Think of your level less as a score and more as a track record. It reflects how much approved, quality work you've completed on the platform — which is exactly the signal that lets our team comfortably open up higher-value, higher-trust opportunities to you as you progress.

This is also why we don't offer any way to manually boost XP outside of actually completing approved tasks. Keeping the level system tied purely to genuine task completion protects what it's meant to represent: real, demonstrated reliability.

## Where This Is Heading

As NexGuild continues to grow, we expect higher levels to unlock increasingly differentiated opportunities — including specialized task categories that require a demonstrated track record before we open them up more broadly. The level system is designed to scale with the platform, rewarding contributors who've invested in building a real history here.

## Final Thoughts

There's no shortcut through the level system, and that's intentional — it exists specifically to reflect genuine, reliable contribution. Start with what's available at your current level, focus on quality over rushing, and you'll find better opportunities opening up naturally as your track record builds. Every experienced contributor on NexGuild started exactly where you are now.
