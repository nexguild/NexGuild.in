If you're new to NexGuild, you might be wondering exactly how the task system works — what counts as a task, how you get paid, and what happens between starting a task and actually earning something from it. This post walks through the entire flow, start to finish.

## What Is a Task on NexGuild?

A task on NexGuild is a defined piece of work posted by our team — anything from a quick data entry job to a multi-step project requiring file uploads, screenshots, or written responses. Each task is created with a clear structure so you always know exactly what's expected before you start.

Every task shows you, upfront:
- A countdown timer showing how long it remains available
- A slots indicator showing how many spots are still open
- The NexCoin reward for completing it
- Any badges relevant to the task — like PRIVATE, FEATURED, or language requirements
- The level required to access it, if applicable

## Browsing Opportunities

The Opportunities page is where you'll find every currently available task. You can filter by task type, payout range, and your current level, and sort by what's newest, highest-paying, or ending soonest.

If a task requires a higher level than you currently have, you'll still see it on the board — it'll show a locked badge with the level requirement clearly marked, rather than disappearing entirely. This way you always know what's coming as you level up, instead of opportunities feeling hidden or mysterious.

## Starting a Task

Before you begin most tasks, you'll see a short Terms & Conditions popup specific to that task — this covers anything important you need to know before committing your time. Once you accept, the task opens into its working view.

## How the Step-by-Step Flow Works

Tasks on NexGuild are built around clear, sequential steps. Rather than dumping all instructions on you at once, each task is broken into stages, and stages unlock one at a time as you complete the one before it. This keeps things focused — you're never juggling five sets of instructions simultaneously.

Each step may ask for a different type of submission depending on what the task requires:
- **Text submission** — for written answers, descriptions, or responses
- **File submission** — for screenshots, documents, or other uploads
- **No submission required** — for steps that are purely instructional (like visiting a link)

A fixed bar at the bottom of the task window keeps your current NexCoin reward and remaining slots visible at all times, so you always know what's at stake as you work through each step.

## Submitting and Review

Once you complete all steps in a task, your submission goes into a review queue. Our review team checks submitted work against the task's requirements before approving it. This protects the integrity of the NexCoin economy — it ensures coins are only awarded for genuinely completed, quality work, which keeps the whole system fair for everyone participating.

## What Happens If a Submission Is Rejected

If your submission doesn't meet the task's requirements, it'll be marked as rejected, and wherever possible, we'll include a reason so you understand what went wrong. Some rejected tasks are eligible for resubmission — you'll see a clear "Retry" option in that case. Others are marked as final, meaning the task can't be resubmitted, and you'll see that clearly labeled too, so there's never confusion about whether you can try again.

## Getting Paid: NexCoins

Once a submission is approved, NexCoins are credited to your balance. NexCoins are NexGuild's in-platform currency — they're not directly cash, but they're redeemable in NexStore for real rewards, including gift cards and vouchers from brands like Amazon, Flipkart, Google Play, and Zomato.

We chose this model deliberately. It lets us keep the platform simple, fast, and focused on rewarding real contribution, without the complexity of handling direct cash transactions for every small task.

## Tracking Your Progress

Your dashboard gives you a real-time view of where you stand: your NexCoins balance, total lifetime earnings, tasks completed, and approval rate. You'll also see your current level, XP progress toward the next level, and a daily streak tracker that rewards consistent activity with bonus NexCoins.

## Why We Built It This Way

The goal behind NexGuild's task system is simple: make it easy to understand exactly what you're doing, why you're doing it, and what you'll get for it — no ambiguity, no hidden steps, no confusion about whether your work counted. Clear structure benefits everyone: contributors know exactly what to expect, and the quality of work stays high because expectations are explicit from the start.

## Ready to Start?

If you haven't already, head over to the Opportunities page and take a look at what's currently available. Start with tasks that match your current level, build up consistent approvals, and you'll find higher-value opportunities opening up as you grow within the platform.
