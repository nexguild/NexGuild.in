If you've seen the name **CPX Research** on a rewards app or earning platform and wondered what it is, you're not alone. The name sounds corporate and not entirely self-explanatory. This guide covers exactly what CPX Research is, how it works, and what to expect from their surveys.

## What Is CPX Research?

**CPX Research is a market research technology company** that operates a survey and offerwall network used by hundreds of earning platforms globally.

The important thing to understand: CPX Research is **not a standalone survey site** where individuals sign up and take surveys directly. They're a B2B (business-to-business) provider. That means they supply survey infrastructure and inventory to other platforms — earning apps, rewards sites, mobile games — that then embed CPX Research surveys into their own product as a feature for users to earn from.

When you complete a CPX Research survey on an earning platform, here's what's actually happening:

1. A brand or research firm commissions a survey through CPX Research's network
2. CPX Research distributes that survey to all their partner platforms
3. You (as a user of one of those platforms) see and complete the survey
4. The research client gets the data they paid for
5. CPX Research pays the platform for verified completions
6. The platform passes a share of that payment to you as coins, points, or cash

This model — advertiser → survey network → platform → user — is how most survey rewards work behind the scenes.

## CPX Research Is Not a Standalone Product

This is the most common point of confusion: **you cannot sign up for CPX Research directly** as a regular user. There's no CPX Research consumer account to create.

What you can do is join an earning platform that has integrated CPX Research. On that platform, the surveys section (often called an "offerwall") will show CPX Research surveys among other earning opportunities.

NexGuild integrates CPX Research directly. When you navigate to the Offerwalls section inside NexGuild and choose CPX Research, you're accessing CPX Research's survey inventory through NexGuild's integration — NexGuild handles your account, your earnings, and your payouts in NexCoins.

## What Types of Surveys Does CPX Research Offer?

CPX Research's survey inventory comes from their advertising clients, which include:

- **Consumer brands** conducting product research
- **Media and entertainment companies** measuring audience opinions
- **Market research agencies** running studies on behalf of their clients
- **App developers** collecting user feedback and usage data

Surveys vary considerably in length and payout. Short surveys (2–5 minutes) are common and pay modest amounts. Longer studies (15–25+ minutes) pay significantly more and typically involve more detailed questions about purchasing behaviour, media consumption, or opinions on specific product categories.

Each survey on CPX Research shows:
- An estimated completion time
- A star rating from previous respondents
- The reward value before you start

The star rating is particularly useful — higher-rated surveys tend to have clearer questions, more accurate time estimates, and fewer mid-survey disqualifications.

## CPX Research and Survey Disqualification

Like all survey networks, CPX Research involves disqualification — being screened out before completing a survey because your demographic profile doesn't match what the study needs.

This is a normal part of how market research surveys work across every provider, not specific to CPX Research. The key factors:

- Every survey targets a specific demographic (age, gender, location, purchase behaviour)
- If you don't match, or if the quota for your profile group is full, you're screened out
- CPX Research often provides a small partial credit for screenout attempts, depending on the platform configuration

The most effective way to reduce disqualification: keep your profile on your earning platform fully and accurately filled out. CPX Research uses your platform profile for pre-screening, so more complete profile data means better survey matching before you start.

## How CPX Research Crediting Works

When you complete a CPX Research survey, they verify the completion quality on their end and send a server-to-server notification (called a postback) to the platform you're using. The platform then credits your account automatically.

On NexGuild, this means your NexCoins appear in your balance within minutes of a verified completion — no manual approval step, no waiting for a review. The automatic crediting is one of CPX Research's more reliable features compared to task-based work that requires human review.

Occasionally, a completion may be later reversed if CPX Research's fraud detection flags it after the fact. This is uncommon for genuine respondents who take their time and answer honestly.
